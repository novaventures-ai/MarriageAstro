## 7. Passive Revenue: AdSense & Mobile Ad Strategy

To maximize earnings with "Less Effort," you can integrate a multi-layered ad strategy that doesn't compromise the premium feel of the app.

### 💰 A. AdSense Optimization (The "SEO Farm" Strategy)
*   **Where to Place:** ONLY on the `/guides/*` and `/blog/*` pages.
*   **Why:** These pages get high-volume organic traffic from people who are "just browsing." Putting ads here captures revenue from 100% of visitors.
*   **Where to Avoid:** The `/report` and `/dashboard` pages. 
*   **Reason:** You want these pages to look professional and expensive so users feel comfortable paying ₹99–₹399. Ads here "cheapen" the brand.

### 🎥 B. The "Watch-to-Unlock" Model (Rewarded Ads)
This is a **Unique Hacker Way** to monetize users who will *never* pay.
*   **The Mechanic:** On the free report, leave one high-value section (e.g., *Sexual Compatibility*) "Blurred."
*   **The Offer:** *"Don't want to pay? Watch a 30-second partner video to unlock this section for 24 hours."*
*   **Implementation:** Use **Google Ad Manager (GAM)** or the **AdSense Offerwall** feature. 
*   **Yield:** Higher RPM than standard banners and significantly increases "Time on Site."

### 🤝 C. Direct Niche Sponsorships (Anti-AdSense)
Instead of random Google ads for socks or sofas, sell custom "Premium Real Estate" in the report footer.
*   **Target:** Local Wedding Photographers, Luxury Venue Owners, or High-end Matrimonial Matchmakers.
*   **Pitch:** *"Your ad will be shown at the exact moment a couple is confirmed to be 'High Compatibility.' It's the most targeted lead you'll ever get."*
*   **Effort:** One-time setup; recurring monthly payout.

### 📱 D. Mobile Ad Platform (AdMob for Hybrid App)
If you eventually wrap the website into a mobile app (using Capacitor or React Native):
*   **AdMob Integration:** Use **Interstitial Ads** only when a user successfully generates a new report (the "Success Moment").
*   **Native Ads:** Integrate ads that look like "Astrological Predictions" within the feed to maintain immersion.
